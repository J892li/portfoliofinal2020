Challenges 


Challenge #1 : Understand and Address the Core themes

Problem:  
We cannot decide on the main direction of our app.

Solution :
The main page always determines the first impression of an app. I have created several main pages to avoid being captured to be too facile to a solution. I considered  three design directions: high contrast, proximity, and alignment. Most important is that those prototypes are iterative and expansive. 


Challenge #2: Design a complex and social-technical page

Problem:  
National Updates, regional policy , and Company Aids are isolated features 

Solution :
My solution focuses on consistency and avoids isolated components. We implemented a Covid -19 API tracker to report real-time data. Design-wise, I used a slider menu and involved a real-time news card section. Users can switch between components easily and having a more consistent experience. I also designed a Covid- 19 inspection form, and the form design will be explained in the next section.

Challenge#3 : Design for better usability

Problem:
Application is long and boring

Solution :
Much of today's application forms are lengthy and complicated. For example, users are not expecting to fill in a work ID or type department name. It will be auto-filled or just menu selection. I also designed two variations in different alignment style for clean and effective user experiences.
